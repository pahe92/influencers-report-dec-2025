üåü CREATOR ECOSYSTEM

Influencer Strategy

Diversified portfolio across mega, macro, micro, and nano influencers for comprehensive market coverage

Content Topics & Campaigns

Strategic content creation across MyBambu's full product suite, driving awareness and adoption:

  • Account Opening: Step-by-step tutorials showcasing the ease of opening a MyBambu account with no credit check required
  • International Money Transfers (IMT): Real-life demonstrations of sending money to family in Latin America with zero fees
  • MyBambu Mastercard: Highlighting card benefits, cashback rewards, and no monthly fees
  • Direct Deposit: Explaining early paycheck access and setting up direct deposit
  • Health Services: Promoting MyBambu's health insurance and telemedicine offerings
  • Bambu Pay: Mobile payment features and peer-to-peer transfers
  • Credits & Loans: Introduction to MyBambu's lending products for credit building
  • Cashback Rewards: Showcasing savings opportunities and reward programs

Geographic Market Expansion

MyBambu's influencer campaigns reached 17+ Latin American markets, building trust and awareness across diverse Hispanic communities:

  • Primary Markets: Venezuela, Mexico, Colombia, Argentina (highest engagement and account sign-ups)
  • Growing Markets: Cuba, Ecuador, Peru, Dominican Republic, Chile
  • Emerging Markets: Nicaragua, Honduras, Guatemala, El Salvador, Panama, Costa Rica, Bolivia, Paraguay
  • U.S. Hispanic Markets: Miami, New York, Los Angeles, Houston, Chicago with bilingual content

üíö OUR MISSION

MyBambu Brand & Purpose

Empowering the unbanked and underbanked Hispanic community with accessible, trustworthy financial services

Who We Serve

MyBambu is a leading Hispanic FinTech company dedicated to providing banking solutions for individuals historically excluded from traditional financial services. We serve the unbanked and underbanked community with friendly, inclusive, and accessible digital banking designed for the Hispanic experience.

Our Value Proposition

  • No Credit Check Banking: Open an account regardless of credit history or immigration status
  • Free International Transfers: Send money to Latin America with $0 fees, competitive exchange rates
  • MyBambu Mastercard: Physical and virtual debit cards with no monthly fees, cashback rewards
  • Early Direct Deposit: Receive paychecks up to 2 days early, instant access to funds
  • Bilingual Support: Full Spanish and English customer service, bilingual app interface
  • Mobile-First Banking: Intuitive app designed for ease-of-use, built for smartphone banking

Why Influencer Marketing Works for MyBambu

  • Trust Building: Hispanic communities rely heavily on personal recommendations and trusted voices
  • Financial Education: Influencers break down complex banking concepts in relatable, accessible language
  • Cultural Relevance: Latino creators understand community values, language nuances, and cultural context
  • Authentic Testimonials: Real users sharing real experiences builds credibility for financial services
  • Viral Reach: Word-of-mouth amplification within tight-knit Hispanic communities
