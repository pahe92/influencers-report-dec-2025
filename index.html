MyBambu Brand & Mission

Empowering the unbanked and underbanked Hispanic community with accessible, trustworthy financial services

Our Mission

MyBambu is a leading Hispanic FinTech company dedicated to providing banking solutions for individuals who have been historically excluded from traditional financial services. We serve the unbanked and underbanked community with friendly, inclusive, and accessible digital banking.

Target Audience

Our primary audience consists of:

  • Hispanic immigrants in the United States seeking accessible banking
  • Latin American residents needing international money transfer services
  • Unbanked individuals looking for their first banking experience
  • Families sending remittances across borders
  • Spanish-speaking communities seeking bilingual financial services

Value Proposition

  • No Credit Check Required: Banking access for everyone, regardless of credit history
  • Free International Transfers: Send money to Latin America with zero fees
  • MyBambu Mastercard: Physical and virtual debit cards with no monthly fees
  • Direct Deposit Services: Receive paychecks up to 2 days early
  • Bilingual Support: Full Spanish and English customer service
  • Mobile-First Experience: Easy-to-use app for all banking needs

Influencer Marketing Goals

  • Awareness: Increase brand recognition within Hispanic communities
  • Trust Building: Leverage influencer credibility to build confidence in financial services
  • Education: Explain banking benefits and features in relatable, accessible language
  • Account Acquisition: Drive new account sign-ups through authentic recommendations
  • Engagement: Foster community conversation around financial empowerment
